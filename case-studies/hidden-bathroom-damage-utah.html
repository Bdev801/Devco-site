Hidden Damage Behind the Walls

Slow leaks often hide behind tile and tubs until demolition begins. When framing or subfloor fails inspection, we stop and rebuild the wet zone the right way — then install finishes that last in Utah conditions.

Discovery

What we often find

Soft subfloor, stained framing, and failed caulking lines are common once surrounds come off. We photograph conditions, explain what requires replacement versus reinforcement, and update scope before ordering finishes.

Repair

Rebuild to a dry baseline

Repairs follow code and manufacturer guidance for wet areas: replaced members tied back to solid structure, ventilation considered, and drainage paths corrected where necessary.

Why skipping repair fails

New tile over damaged structure guarantees callbacks. We rebuild so your warranty on workmanship actually means something.

Waterproofing

Ready for finish materials

Once repairs pass inspection, we install the waterproofing system as a continuous assembly — corners, penetrations, and bench transitions detailed before tile or panels cover them.
