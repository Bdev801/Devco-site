Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your remodel.

Most bathroom remodels in Salt Lake County range from $8,000–$25,000+ depending on size, materials and scope. A basic refresh runs lower; a full gut with custom tile shower or Sentrel system runs higher. We provide detailed, itemized free estimates — no surprises.
Sentrel by Design Imaging is a premium shower wall panel system — seamless, grout-free and waterproof. It looks like luxury stone, installs faster than tile, and requires zero grout maintenance. It's a great option if you want a high-end look with less upkeep. We install both and can help you choose the right fit for your project and budget.
Yes — this is one of our most requested services. We convert standard bathrooms into fully accessible spaces for seniors, wheelchair users and those with mobility challenges. This includes walk-in showers, grab bars, fold-down bench seats, low threshold bases and handheld shower systems. We make the conversion safe and beautiful at the same time.
A typical bathroom remodel takes 1–3 weeks. A full gut renovation with custom tile usually takes 2–4 weeks. Sentrel installations are often faster. We'll give you a clear timeline before work begins and keep you updated throughout.
Yes. Devco Construction LLC is fully licensed as a contractor in Utah and carries full general liability insurance. We're also BBB Accredited. We're happy to provide proof of license and insurance before any work begins.
