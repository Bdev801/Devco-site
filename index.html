Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your remodel.

Most bathroom remodels in Salt Lake County range from $8,000–$25,000+ depending on size, materials and scope of work. A basic refresh (tile, vanity, fixtures) runs lower; a full gut with custom tile shower runs higher. We provide detailed, itemized free estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for — no surprises.
A typical bathroom remodel takes 1–3 weeks. A full gut renovation with custom tile, waterproofing, and new fixtures usually takes 2–4 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before work begins and keep you updated throughout the project.
