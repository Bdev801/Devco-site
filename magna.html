Utah Hard Water Hits Magna Hard

Magna sits in a particularly hard water zone in Salt Lake County. The mineral content coming out of Magna taps is tough on standard grout — especially in shower environments that see daily use. We use epoxy grout on every shower installation because it holds up to Utah water without cracking, discoloring, or requiring annual resealing. That's what we'd put in our own homes.
